Affine Global

The Affine Global Corporation
is a consortium of entrepreneurs.

IBM Global Entrepreneur

The Affine Network is a consortium of entrepreneurs, institutional investors and technology professionals who share a common purpose. This is to use advanced technology to create a global, hyperfast, compliant platform for the trading of digital assets.

Digital assets

These assets include securities, items of intangible value such as digital rights and highly sensitive information such as personal identity.
We do business through partnerships with large, international corporations, trading firms and asset managers.

Our role

Our role is to identify opportunities in existing markets and to create new ones by applying a deep understanding of both local custom and technological change.
We believe the arrival of the knowledge economy will small, agile network companies like ours to punch above our weight.

Power of Five

Our strategy of the Power of Five is a complement to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.
It is a good business to offer a market of nearly two billion people basic financial services, clean energy, education on demand, a decentralization of ownership to local communities and a new, transparent and efficient way of investing in frontier states.
