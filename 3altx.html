AltX Africa

ALTX is the product of a vision to establish a set of fully interconnected markets in Africa that have full access to each other.
In order to fulfill this vision, ALTX applied and obtained its first exchange and depository licenses in Uganda in partnership with GMEX group in 2014 and 2015.
